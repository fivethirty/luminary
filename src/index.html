About

Luminary is a battle calculator for Eclipse: Second Dawn for the Galaxy.

Targeting

Luminary assumes that AI follow the targeting rules for AI ships and that damage will be stacked on previously damaged ships.

Player ships are assumed to remove as much enemy damage as possible with their shots (they ignore missile damage after a ship has fired its missiles).
