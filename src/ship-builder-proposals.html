What stays consistent

  • Faction traits sit in a locked Built in rail and never consume a square.
  • Used discovery tiles remain visible with their current location; they can be moved, not duplicated.
  • Muon Source uses a separate external socket and is still part of the one-copy discovery inventory.
  • Every proposal keeps energy, initiative, movement, defenses, and weapons visible while editing.
